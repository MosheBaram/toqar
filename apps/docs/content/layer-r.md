# R — Retention & Expansion

Do they keep delegating? `weekly_task_actors` replaces DAU with the count
of accounts running tasks. `task_depth_expansion` tracks distinct task
types per account over time; `delegation_share` is the agent-initiated
vs. human-initiated split; `net_task_growth` is week-over-week completed
tasks per account. Expansion is measured in delegation, not seats.
